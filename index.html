What is Remember about?

A world without dreams. A girl who remembers.

Remember by Cheyenne Cleveland is a YA dystopian sci-fi novel set after the Last War, in a world where humanity has lost the ability to dream.

Inside the United Regime’s concrete walls, Reyna carries a secret that could change everything. While the rest of the world sleeps in darkness, she dreams of a life before Earth’s ruin. But Reyna’s dreams do not just reveal the truth about humanity’s forgotten past — they make her a target.

Haunting, cinematic, faith-enriched, and emotionally intense, Remember follows Reyna’s journey to uncover the truth buried beneath lies and awaken a sleeping world.
